## Summary

Binary IRT models differ in whether they estimate item difficulty only, item discrimination, or lower asymptote/guessing. They should not be applied directly to five-point Likert responses without recoding rationale and loss-of-information review.

## Checks

- Confirm the response format is binary before recommending 1PL, 2PL, or 3PL.
- Check dimensionality and local independence before interpreting item parameters.
- Treat model choice as a substantive and empirical decision, not just a software default.

## Use When

- The user has dichotomous scored items.
- The user asks whether Rasch, 2PL, or 3PL is appropriate.
