## Summary

Ordered Likert items can be examined with polytomous IRT models such as the graded response model or generalized partial credit model when dimensionality and local independence are defensible.

## Checks

- Check category ordering and sparse categories before calibration.
- Review item discrimination, threshold ordering, item fit, and information curves.
- Use IRT as a sensitivity analysis unless sample size and model assumptions are strong.

## Use When

- The study uses ordered Likert or rating-scale items.
- The user asks for item-level diagnostics beyond CTT reliability.
