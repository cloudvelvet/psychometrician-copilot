## Summary

IRT item fit statistics are diagnostic signals. Poor fit may reflect multidimensionality, local dependence, category problems, sample mismatch, or item wording rather than a simple delete/keep decision.

## Checks

- Review dimensionality and local-dependence diagnostics before interpreting item fit.
- Combine statistical flags with expert item review.
- Document whether flagged items are revised, removed, retained, or sent to sensitivity analysis.

## Use When

- IRT is recommended or requested.
- The user asks how to act on item fit warnings.
