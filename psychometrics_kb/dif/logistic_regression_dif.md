## Summary

Logistic regression DIF models item response as a function of trait proxy, group, and their interaction. It can screen for uniform group differences and nonuniform group-by-trait effects.

## Checks

- Include the matching trait proxy before testing group effects.
- Inspect both statistical significance and effect size.
- For ordered categories, use an ordinal approach or justify the scoring transformation.

## Use When

- The user asks about uniform or nonuniform DIF.
- A flexible item-level fairness screen is needed before expert review.
