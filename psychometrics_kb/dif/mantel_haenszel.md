## Summary

Mantel-Haenszel DIF compares item performance across groups after matching on a total score or trait proxy. It is often used as an interpretable screening method, especially for binary or suitably categorized items.

## Checks

- Define focal and reference groups before running the screen.
- Use a defensible matching variable and inspect sample size within score strata.
- Treat DIF flags as prompts for expert review, not automatic evidence of bias.

## Use When

- The user requests item-level fairness screening.
- The item format and grouping design fit Mantel-Haenszel assumptions.
