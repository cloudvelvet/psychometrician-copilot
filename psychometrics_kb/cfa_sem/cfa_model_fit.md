## Summary

CFA fit indices help diagnose whether a hypothesized measurement model is plausible, but they do not validate a scale by themselves. Fit should be interpreted with loadings, residuals, competing models, theory, sample features, and response format.

## Checks

- Inspect factor loadings, residual correlations, standard errors, and inadmissible estimates.
- Report plausible alternative models rather than repairing fit only through modification indices.
- Avoid rigid CFI/RMSEA/SRMR cutoffs as automatic pass/fail decisions.

## Use When

- A hypothesized factor structure is supplied.
- The user asks whether CFA results are acceptable.
